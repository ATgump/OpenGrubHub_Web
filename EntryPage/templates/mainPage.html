Welcome to OpenGrubHub

As the population dramatically increases and people tend to try various types of food from different countries, the demand for restaurants seems to increase as well. However, people sometimes have fewer channels to reach the information of different restaurants. Even if they are able to do it, they may have some problems like a long queue, business hours, price of food, questions asking, and so on. Similarly, if people want to hold a big party, they would like to plan ahead and book an appointment, but the missing phone call and time-limited may come under straitened circumstances. Besides, the weather is an important factor which causes people to want to have a delivery order. Fortunately, in this day and age of digitalization, our new killer app Open Grub Table Hub is an ideal app to make people’s lives more convenient and time-saving.
